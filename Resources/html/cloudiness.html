CLOUDINESS

In this visual, we see the effects that latitude has upon cloudiness in our sample cities.


  • Clouds appear to be spread evenly regardless of the latitude of the sample cities.

  • The sample concentrates more greatly on high and low cloud percentages with less concentration in the middle range, rather than by latitude.

    • In this area of study, there appears to be no strong impact of latitide on the cloudiness of our sample cities.
