WIND SPEED

In this visual, we see the effects that latitude has upon wind speed in our sample cities.


  • Northern latitudes have a higher number of cities where wind speed increases on average.

  • The wind speed is generally low in the total sample of cities. High winds affect fewer cities.

  • The southern latitude cities tend to have higher winds than the majority of cities, but only barely. The North is more affected by winds.

    • Latitide is not a significant factor in whether the average wind speed is higher, but cities with the highest wind speeds tend to be in the northern hemisphere closest to the pole.


    This could be due to many factors including the location of sample cities, various climates, or elevations where these cities are located.
