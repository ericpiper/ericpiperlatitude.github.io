THE IMPACT OF WHERE YOU ARE

When you have a moment, take a look at the sky. Count the clouds. Feel the wind on your clothes. Is it dry? Is it hot? Think about where you are and how that might affect what you are experiencing.

Latitude has an effect on many aspects of weather. In this project, we will take a closer look how the weather is affected by latitude. With a cross-section of over 500 cities, we examine the effects of all of the your location relative to the poles and see whether what you experience is affected by where you are.

Here's what we will examine the effects of latitude on the following:

  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Cloudiness
  • Wind Speed
